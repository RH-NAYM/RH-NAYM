### 👋 About Me

I'm the **Head of AI at HawkEyes Digital Monitoring Ltd.** in Dhaka, Bangladesh, where I own applied-AI delivery end to end — research through production deployment — across retail-execution, manufacturing, and surveillance product lines.

I've spent 3+ years shipping AI systems that run at national scale: a computer-vision platform serving **2M+ retail outlets at ~99% accuracy** for enterprise clients (BAT, Unilever, Nagad), production **RAG and multi-agent systems**, a **real-time WebRTC voice agent**, and low-resource **Bangla OCR/TTS** models. I also design and run our **two self-hosted on-prem Ubuntu GPU servers**, because I'd rather own the infrastructure than rent it.

- 🔭 **Currently building:** a custom Bangladeshi-accent Bangla TTS model (VITS + grapheme frontend) to power AI voice agents for inbound/outbound call automation (hospital scheduling, sales, support).
- 🧠 **Day to day:** production CV (YOLO detection/segmentation), RAG & agentic LLM systems, GPU-optimized async inference, and MLOps.
- 🐧 **Daily driver:** Arch Linux + Hyprland (3+ years), on-prem GPU servers I built myself.
- 📦 **55+ repositories** across 8 AI domains · **50+ personal / 11+ organizational** Hugging Face model repos.
- 🎓 B.Sc. Electrical & Electronic Engineering, BUBT (2021).
- 💬 Ask me about: retail-execution CV pipelines, production RAG architecture, real-time voice agents, or self-hosting GPU infrastructure.
